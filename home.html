Who We Are

Reader Society is a group of homeschoolers who share the same passion in reading books. We believe in reading as a shared adventure, one that brings imagination and connection to our inner child.

From fantasy sags to amazing graphic novels, our events blend storytelling with experience. Come find your next favorite book — and the people who’ll love it with you.
